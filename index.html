Ryan Amos

Hello! I'm a PhD candidate at Princeton. My PhD advisors are Prateek Mittal and Ed Felten. My most recent work has focused on longitudinal internet measurements to address and understand consumer protection issues. I'm generally interested in cryptography, security, privacy, and tech policy. Additionally, I have experience in computational genomics and computational immunology.

I run the security and privacy reading group at Princeton. If you're interested, please send me an email.

In my spare time, I run the Princeton University Blacksmiths, I'm a director for the New Jersey Blacksmiths Association, I climb rocks and teach rock climbing, I play the guitar, and I ferment things (usually intentionally).

My publications and ongoing projects can be found on my CV (link).
